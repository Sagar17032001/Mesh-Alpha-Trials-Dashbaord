Cumulative Fleet Analytics
Band Distribution (TOT) ap_with_poor_tot_on_5G: Total usage on 5G / (total usage on 5G + 2G). Shows the band split across all clients.
Client Capability (Best) Shows each unique client's best WiFi capability observed across all records. Hierarchy: WiFi 6E (EHT) > WiFi 6 (HE) > WiFi 5 (AC/VHT) > WiFi 4 (HT) > Legacy.
Pod Health Status ap_downtime_per: AP downtime percentage. Pods with >50% downtime are classified as Offline.
Poor Coverage ap_with_poor_coverage_2: % samples where signal < -80dBm / total samples (2G).
ap_with_poor_coverage_5: % samples where signal < -80dBm / total samples (5G).
Poor SNR ap_with_poor_snr_sample_2: Samples where SNR <10 / Total samples (2G).
ap_with_poor_snr_sample_5: Samples where SNR <10 / Total samples (5G).
Poor MCS Rate ap_with_poor_link_speed_mcs_rate: Count of stations where Tx link < 65 Mbps. A station qualifies if MCS rate < 65 Mbps for >50% of reported samples.
Sticky Clients Sticky 2G: Clients with VHT/EHT/HE capability and signal <-56 on 2G band.
Sticky 5G: Clients with HT capability and signal >-80 on 5G band.
Client Disconnections ap_with_high_client_disconnection: Count of stations having high disconnection. A station qualifies if dissociation events received > 20.
Pod Uptime ap_with_low_uptime: Duration between cloud connection alarm starting and ending / 24. If duration > 24 hours, KPI reported as 100.
